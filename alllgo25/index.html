Sovereign AI runtime

Alllgo-25

Alllgo-25 is a sovereign architecture built to remain owner-bound: governed autonomy, non-bypassable authority, and evidence-grade audit trails.

Core idea

A powerful assistant isn’t defined by what it can do — but by what it is allowed to do, under what conditions, and with what proof.

Immutable authority core

Foundational directives + integrity checks define the boundary. The system cannot “self-exempt.”

Governance & policy gate

Every intent is evaluated for risk, permissions, and required warrants/consent.

Autonomy tiers

Safe, graduated autonomy. Higher-impact actions escalate to explicit operator approval.

How it behaves

  • Proposes actions with clear intent and impact.
  • Requests approval for side-effects and sensitive operations.
  • Logs decisions, policy outcomes, and execution evidence.
  • Refuses to bypass governance, even if prompted.

What “sovereign” means here

Explicit boundaries, and the operator as the final authority. No vendor-controlled runtime decisions. No silent data exfiltration.
